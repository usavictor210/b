General Information

Numbers is an idle incremental game.

Keyboard shortcuts

Press 1-7 to buy max of a number, m to max all, d to buy max doublings, c to click, i to infinity, s to shift.
Challenges are partially implemented, but aren't done yet.
There are plans for autobuyers, but implementation hasn't really been started yet.
The numbers you formed have become mingled with each other and formed something greater...
